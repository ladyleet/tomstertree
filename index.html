Ember Sherpa Tomster.


You can find Ember Sherpa tweeting on Twitter @EmberSherpa! He is from Toronto and loves contributing to open source! I find him rather helpful.

In case you ever want to bribe him to help you with something, Ember Sherpa loves cuban cigars and whiskey. We went skydiving once with @michaellnorth. Ember Sherpa was not scared.

Ember London Tomster


London Tomster was the first tomster I ever met IRl and fell in love with. He is so cute, drinking tea and all. London Tomster mainly hangs with his bestie @jgwhite at @withassociates all day, writing teh embers. He's a great community leader!

London Tomster can be found at the Ember London meetups! His fave meetups are project nights. Especially when sponsors come and bring yummy food. You can tweet him at @EmberLondon!

Q and A with London Tomster

Built With Ember Tomster


This is Built With Ember Tomster! He loves legos and is from Puerrrrrrrto Riiiiicooooo and eats tacos de pescado all the time! Built With Ember Tomster hangs mainly with Giovanni @gcollazo and Jose @jpadilla_. He has a super cool site too called Built With Ember.

Ember Munich Tomster


Ember Munich Tomster hangs out in Germany at Ember meetups and can be found tweeting @EmberjsMunich! Munich Tomsters is pretty tight with Simplabs, which means we think Simplabs is pretty cool too.

Ember Uruguay Tomster


We love Ember Uruguay Tomster because he loves to share! Uruguay Tomster also loves futbol when he's not coding or tweeting, but most of the time he's hanging out on Twitter and at a Ember Montevideo meetup spreading the love of ember.

One more important thing if you ever run into this tomster! One of Uruguay Tomster's fave things to share is mate, which is a national drink! Yummy!

Ember Jax Tomster


Who wouldn't want to chill down at the beach at with Tomster Jacksonville-Beach! We're jealous of one of Tomster Jax's besties Chase who gets to hang all the time. Never fear though, if you want to converse with Tomster Jax, just find him on Twitter.

Tomster Jax has a super awesome meetup that hosts project nights too! If you're in town, you should come! Sign up here.

Ember Austin Tomster


With his big 'ol cowboy hat and guitar, Ember Austin Tomster is the musical one out of the bunch! He'll woo you with country songs about javascript and teach you how to code through his lyrics!

You can peek at what he's up to on Twitter @EmberATX or meet him IRL (or in sticker form) at an Ember ATX Meetup! Tell him we say hi!

Ember Seattle Tomster


The first Tomster with a neck beard! Tomster Seattle loves his coffee and plaid shirts. He can be found hanging out at the Ember Seattle meetups.

Benjamin Franklin Tomster for Ember Philly


This Tomster has got the smarts on him! You can find him at Ember Philly meetups!
Benjamin Franklin Tomster's FAVE ember add-on is ember-paper because he invented the printing press! AND he LOVES apple pie!
